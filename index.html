Data Scientist

Building AI solutions on the Edge.

I am a PhD candidate at Loughborough University based in the UK. My PhD research topic is 'Computationally Efficient Embedded Classification Algorithms for Augmentative and Alternative Communication (AAC)'. I work on researching efficient AI solutions for assistive technologies that help individuals who are speech disabled. Partnering with me is the Ace Centre, a charity that deals with assistive technologies and AAC. I hold a master's degree (with distinction) in Data Science awarded at Loughborough University, as well as a bachelor's degree (first class honours) in Electrical and Electronic Engineering. My passion lies in working with artificial intelligence to make a positive impact in society.
